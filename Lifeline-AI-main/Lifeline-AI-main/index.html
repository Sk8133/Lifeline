Core Features

Everything you need in an emergency

A complete emergency decision platform built with real data and intelligent logic.

Smart Navigation
Uses your GPS location and OpenStreetMap Overpass API to find real hospitals within 7km radius in real time.
AI Symptom Analysis
Describe your symptoms and get instant AI triage: urgency level, likely condition, recommended department, and action steps.
Blood Availability
Real-time blood bank data showing all blood groups (A+, B+, O-, AB+ etc.) across nearby hospitals with unit counts.
Live Interactive Map
Powered by Leaflet.js and OpenStreetMap. See your location and all hospitals pinned on a live map with popup details.
Decision Engine
Intelligent ranking algorithm weighs distance, wait time, and availability to recommend the single best hospital for your situation.
Doctor Availability
Each hospital card shows estimated doctors on duty, wait time, and bed availability — helping you choose wisely under pressure.
How It Works

Three steps to emergency clarity

1
Describe Symptoms
Type what you're feeling. Our AI analyzes keywords and severity to classify urgency and suggest a department.
2
Find Hospitals
We use your GPS + OpenStreetMap to find real hospitals within 7km and display them on an interactive live map.
3
Get Best Recommendation
Our decision engine ranks all hospitals by distance + wait time + availability and shows you the single best option.
